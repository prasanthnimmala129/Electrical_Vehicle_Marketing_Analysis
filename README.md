# Electrical Vehicle Marketing Analysis

**Problem Statement:**
Electrical Vehicle Marketing Analysis – find ways to increase EV sales and attract more customers.

**Dataset Link**: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/srinrealyf/india-ev-market-data/

**1. Market Trends:** Study how EV sales and demand are growing.

**2. Charging Infrastructure:** Understand charging station availability and needs.

**3. Segmentation Analysis:** Group customers based on their needs and choices.

**4. Data Visualization:** Present data using simple charts for better understanding.
